Cliffs of Moher

The first stop will be the Cliffs of Moher. You can get there by tourist bus from Dublin in about two and a half hours. The Cliffs of Moher are considered a national treasure of Ireland. Located on the shores of the Atlantic Ocean, in County Clare. If you go there on a tour with a group, then you are given a couple of hours of free time and take my word for it, you will enjoy every single minute. Fabulous landscapes and the large scale of the Cliffs will not leave anyone indifferent. After all these views and landscapes, you will definitely get hungry, so let's move on to our next stop!
