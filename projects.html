Fitz's Bar

A few kilometers from the Cliffs of Moher in the small town of Doolin, there is a very good restaurant with European cuisine. A good atmosphere, caring staff and not very high prices will take care of you there. Anyone can find a dish to their preference. After you grab some food, our last stop awaits us!
