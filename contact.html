Galway

Our last stop will be a port city called Galway. It is an amazingly beautiful city with its own culture and history. It is not for nothing that the harp is depicted on the coat of arms of Ireland; it represents musical the culture of the country. In the center of Galway, you will find a music artist in almost every corner who entertains people with his or her music. The city is literally saturated with various sounds of musical instruments. That is why a visit to this city will be the perfect ending of your trip day!
